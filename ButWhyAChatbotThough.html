Why A Chatbot ?

As you know very well, sometimes I can be a bullshitter.

Whether we’re talking about how I went about elements of our relationship or the times when I’ve fallen asleep with you while in the library, my actions have not always been up to par with my true intentions.

Building this was something that I didn’t want to bullshit. It’s something that my body and mind would not let me bullshit. I created this chatbot to not only house my plans, actions, and guides for how I intend to go about rebuilding and growing our relationship, but more importantly, to serve as an embodiment of change.

This is my first website* and there is no one else I would rather have created it for.

*something that could not have been accomplished while bullshitting

I wanted to create something that would:

  • Communicate to you the ways I have changed and how serious I am about continuing along this path

  • Express my deepest feelings and thoughts I have about you, us, and what I want our relationship to be

  • Detail to you the action-oriented plans that reinforce my pursuit for continued growth for both myself and our relationship

  • Set a precedent for how I intend to do the right thing the first time for all things we have yet to experience

  • Convey to you that despite knowing I needed to respect your space after Dec 26th, I haven’t gotten over you one bit, but have grown so much since.
